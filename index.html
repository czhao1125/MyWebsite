About Me ♡

Welcome to my website. Let me start by introducting myself. I am Crystal, a second year combined major of computer science and statistics student at UBC, who wants to be a software developer.

More About Me

Hi, this is still Crystal. Some may ask, why software developer? I have always been intrigued by solving difficult problems since I was little. And my father, who graduated from Computer Science at U of T and became a programmer, would always "brainwash" me on how computer science is better and more interesting than any other fields of study. I, of course, did not believe him. However, I still decided that I will take a few computer science courses during my first year to try it out. After taking the courses, it quickly captured my heart, and that is when I realized, I want to become a software developer, just like my dad. In addition, some part of me also believed that I got the genes for computer science, so it might be easier for me to learn CS than to learn ECON.

A little fun fact about me, I love drawing and painting. Although I have always went on and on about how I want to become a software developer that works at Google or Microsoft or Amazon or Facebook or basically any big fancy companies, I actually love drawing and painting more than coding. That is the first thing or in fact, the only thing, I am good at. I started drawing and painting since little, but my mom always thought piano is better, so I took piano classes every week, instead of art classes.
